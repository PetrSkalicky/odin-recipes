Old Fashioned Potato Salad recipe

Old Fashioned Potato Salad with a thousands positive reviews

There are many ways to make potato salad, but if you are looking for a traditional, mayonnaise-based potato salad made with hard-boiled eggs, celery and a little pickle relish for zing, you have landed on the right recipe.

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle ralish
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
  • ground black pepper to taste

Recipe steps

  1. Cook potatoes, after that drain, cool,peel and chop them
  2. While potatoes cook, place eggs in boiling water for 5min after that remove them from hot water, cool, peel and chop them
  3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Mix together well and refigerate until chilled
